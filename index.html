    About Spring

    Overview

    Spring Education Center is an educational institution founded by a team with over 15 years of experience in education sector. Spring was established to educate and train high school students, undergraduates, postgraduates, businesspeople, and working professionals in the private, public, non-governmental organization, and non-profit organization sectors wanting to build capacities for using formal languages (English & Chinese) and stronger business acumen in professional settings.
